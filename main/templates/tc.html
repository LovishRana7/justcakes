Terms and Conditions

1. Payments

  • a. All payments for cake orders must be made online through our secure payment gateway.
  • b. Customers have the option to make either full payment at the time of ordering or pay 50% of the total amount and the remaining balance on the day of pick-up.
  • c. The remaining balance can be paid either through Cash on Delivery (COD) or online payment methods available on our website.
  • d. Orders will be placed and confirmed only after the payment has been successfully verified.

2. Order Cancellation and Refunds

  • a. In the event of order cancellation, it must be done within 12 hours of making the payment to be eligible for a full refund.
  • b. If the cancellation is made within 12 hours, the entire amount paid will be refunded.
  • c. If the cancellation is made after 12 hours, the refund will be subject to the following conditions:
    • - If half payment has been made, a 30% cancellation fee will be deducted from the amount paid, and the remaining balance will be refunded.
    • - If full payment has been made, a 50% cancellation fee will be deducted from the amount paid, and the remaining balance will be refunded.
  • d. Refunds will be processed through the original payment method used during the order.

3. Order Pick-up

  • a. Customers are required to pick up their cake orders from our store location.
  • b. The exact pick-up location and time will be provided upon order confirmation.
  • c. It is the customer's responsibility to ensure timely pick-up of their orders.

4. Quality and Customization

  • a. We strive to provide high-quality cakes that meet or exceed customer expectations.
  • b. Customizations, including flavors, designs, and inscriptions, can be requested at the time of placing the order.
  • c. While we make every effort to accommodate customization requests, we reserve the right to make necessary adjustments based on feasibility and available resources.

  • c. In the event of unforeseen circumstances that prevent us from fulfilling an order, we will make every effort to notify the customer and provide a suitable alternative or a refund.
